The Problem

The shelf edge is the most valuable
surface in retail. It still runs by hand.

The shelf is where the purchase decision happens. But pricing updates take hours of manual labor, promotions sit behind paper nobody reads, and the space generates zero media revenue.

Pricing errors cost margin

Manual tag changes are slow, labor-intensive, and frequently inaccurate. Every mismatch between your POS and your shelf is money lost.

Static promotions get ignored

Paper shelf talkers and printed signage are easy to overlook, hard to update, and impossible to measure. Your best offers go unnoticed.

Shelf space earns nothing

Your shelf edges have attention value, but no way to monetize it. Brands want point-of-purchase influence — you have no channel to sell it.

Labor drains operations

Every price change, every promo swap, every seasonal reset requires staff time on the floor. That labor has a compounding cost most operators underestimate.

The Solution

One platform. Pricing, promotion, and media — all at the shelf.

Retail Lumina gives you centralized, real-time control over what your shelves communicate. Update pricing across every category instantly. Push targeted promotions to high-margin or overstock items. And open your shelf edges as a media channel where brands pay to reach shoppers at the moment of decision.

It's not signage. It's a pricing and revenue platform built for the shelf edge.
