Experiences & Activations

The shelf screen is more than
a price tag.

Once the shelf is live, brands and retailers can unlock a new class of in-store experience — from gamified shopper engagement to full campaign activations that turn a gondola aisle into a branded environment.

Gamification

Make shopping a game.

The shelf screen gives brands a live canvas to reward curiosity, attention, and loyalty with mechanics that go far beyond a static coupon.

  • In-aisle trivia and product knowledge challenges with instant rewards
  • Multi-aisle or multi-store scavenger hunts that drive full-store traffic
  • Loyalty-linked milestones — scan, engage, and earn in a single visit
  • Time-limited flash challenges that create urgency at the shelf
  • QR-triggered activations that bridge the screen to the shopper’s phone
Brand Activations

Own the aisle, not just the label.

Brands can move beyond the product package and take control of the entire shelf environment — with dynamic, coordinated creative delivered at the exact point of purchase.

  • Product launch takeovers that animate the full category section on day one
  • Seasonal campaign pods with coordinated creative across relevant aisles
  • Motion brand storytelling at the moment the shopper is comparing options
  • Cross-sell and bundle messaging triggered contextually next to key SKUs
  • Network-wide campaign delivery — update every screen in every store instantly

Shelf Trivia

Shoppers answer a brand-sponsored question right at the shelf and unlock a discount or loyalty points on the spot.

Scavenger Hunt

A clue on one shelf screen sends shoppers to another aisle, building basket size and dwell time across the entire store.

Launch Takeover

A brand pays to animate every screen in their section during a product launch week — coordinated motion across the full gondola bay.

Loyalty Reward

A QR code on the shelf screen links directly to the retailer’s loyalty app — shopper scans, purchases, and earns points without leaving the aisle.
